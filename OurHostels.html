About Our Hostels

We offer two hostels, Boys Hostel and a Girls Hostel, each tailored to different lifestyles. Boys Hostel is a vibrant and social space, perfect for those who enjoy group activities and making new friends, fostering a lively community atmosphere. In contrast, Girls Hostel provides a calm and secure environment, ideal for those who prefer privacy and a peaceful stay. Both hostels ensure comfort, safety, and modern amenities, creating a welcoming home away from home. We also provide the facility of an excellent Mess with a minimum of 4 meals served throughout the day.
